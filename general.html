General

General



Turbo Dynamics has become one of the most reputable manufacturers of gas turbine replacement parts with the most competitive prices. We manufacture and supply all hot section parts, buckets, nozzles, transition pieces and cap and liners. We use the same materials, manufacturing process and coatings that are used by OEM.

Having the highest technological know how, Turbo Dynamics is among the very few companies in the world such as GE and Rolls Royce who have been approved by the Federal Aviation Administration and the United States Air Force for manufacturing the most sophisticated parts (blades, vanes, thermocouples) for jet engines, both military and commercial.

Our engineers design, verify and validate parts that equal or exceed OEM standards while drastically reducing our product to market development cycle in compliance with ISO 9001:2008. With over three decades of supplying parts which represent millions of aggregate turbine operating hours, TDC's vast engineering experience has established us as a market leader in supplying non OEM replacement parts for gas turbines and jet engines.
