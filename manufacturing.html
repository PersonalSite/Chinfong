Manufacturing

Manufacturing



The manufacturing and supplying capabilities of Turbo Dynamics are as diversified as the customers we support. We manufacture high-precision parts that fit in the palm of your hand to parts exceeding 200 lbs that are mounted into large-duty industrial land based gas turbines.



The company has a state of the art manufacturing facility on Long Island, New York and we are one of the very few manufacturing companies in the world who have our own ECM machineries and capabilities. Our facilities offer CNC conventional machining, surface grinding, a complete line of ECM (Electro Chemical Machining method), EDM, cooling holes capabilities for turbine blades, vane segments and replacement airfoil coupons, coating and X-ray inspection.

Precision parts which TDC supplies include:

Vane Segment Assemblies
Seal Housing Assemblies
Ring Segments
Blades (Buckets)
Nozzles
Transition Assemblies
Combustion Liners
Fuel Atomizers (Nozzles)
Instrument Panel Components
Fuel Pumps
Circulating Pumps (auxilary or main), etc.
